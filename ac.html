ABOUT - This particular web site is devoted primarily to the John Deere precision replica lines. The Precision Classic lines are some of the most highly detailed replicas of farm machinery ever produced. Listed on the main page you will find a brief description of all the John Deere Precision Classic releases. In the original Precision Classic line there are 25 numbered models in the 14 year production run which started in 1990. You will also find a few special editions and variations. Throughout the years Ertl has also issued some other precisions that were not necessarily part of the original product line. These additional lines include a smaller 1/32 scale line referred to as the Series II Precision Classic line, A Precision Classic Construction line of equipment, a series of Precision Classic Lawn and Garden tractors and equipment, and a new line of precisions introduced in late 2004 marketed as the Precision Key series and finally in 2011 the Precision Elite Series. On the home page each edition will list available inventory and retail pricing. Since these collectors items are not toys you will find the prices may vary greatly depending on several points. The age of the item, the rarity of the item, condition of the replica, the completeness of the replica and the condition of the box. Therefore, inventory may vary in price accordingly. Please keep in mind that the inventory may not necessarily be new. Many items I may purchase second hand because of the length of time that they have been discontinued. Each item is opened for inspection (unless the package is factory sealed), its condition is carefully evaluated, it is returned to its original packing and notations are made along with each listing.
